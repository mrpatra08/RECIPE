Simple Omlette Recipe

An easy and quik dish , perfect for any meal . This classis omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, opyionally filled with your choice of cheese , vegetables, or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 mins
  • Preparation: 5 mins
  • Cooking: 5 mins

Ingrediants

  • 2-3 Large eggs

  • Salt, to taste

  • Pepper, to taste

  • 1 tablespoon od butter or oil

  • Optional fillings: cheese,diced vegetables, cooked meats , hearbs


Instructions

  1. beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for fluffier texture.


  2. Heat the pan: PLace a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.


  3. Cook the omlette: once thee butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the panto ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.


  4. Add fillings (Optional): when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen filings over one half of the omlette.


  5. Fold and Serve:As the omlette continuos to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filling. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.


  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
